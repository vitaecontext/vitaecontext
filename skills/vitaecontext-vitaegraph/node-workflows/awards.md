# Award node workflow

Load this workflow when sources mention awards, placements, scholarships, competitive recognition, or formal honors.

Store records at `awards/<award-slug>.md`. Use `type: award` and `id: award:<stable-slug>`.

Capture issuer, date, geographic or competitive scope, selection criteria, result, recognized work, team context, and significance. Link the project, publication, degree, or role that earned the recognition.

Do not upgrade participation into placement, a category award into an overall win, or local recognition into broader scope. Preserve ambiguity as a focused question.
