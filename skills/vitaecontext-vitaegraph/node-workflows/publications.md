# Publication node workflow

Load this workflow for papers, articles, conference proceedings, posters, talks with published records, preprints, or substantial public research outputs.

Store records at `publications/<publication-slug>.md`. Use `type: publication` and `id: publication:<stable-slug>`.

Capture the official title, authorship order, venue, date, identifier, status, accessible summary, method, result, personal contribution, related research, and public links. Link thesis, project, education, and experience records where appropriate.

Distinguish published, accepted, submitted, preprint, poster, and presentation states. Do not infer peer review or publication status from a manuscript alone.
